The Spike Engine

Spike Terminal automates the entire discovery pipeline. It ingests price data, scrapes real-time news via Playwright, runs FinBERT sentiment analysis, and outputs a DistilBART summary in seconds.

Terminal Capabilities

A layered signal engine designed for precision and speed.

Financial NLP Engine

Utilizes FinBERT to accurately gauge the sentiment of breaking financial news, specifically trained on market vernacular to distinguish between noise and material impact.

DistilBART Summarization

Don't read 10 articles. The backend automatically condenses multiple lengthy financial reports into a single, high-signal paragraph explaining the price action.

Headless Scraping

Powered by Playwright (Chromium) to bypass static roadblocks and extract dynamic, real-time news data from major financial portals the second a spike occurs.

Technical Aggregation

Instantly pulls moving averages, RSI, and volume profiles, overlaying them against the fundamental news data for a complete picture.

Sector Clustering

Groups stocks by sector and historical correlation to determine if a spike is idiosyncratic (company-specific) or systemic (sector-wide).

Reactive Frontend

A sleek, Bloomberg-inspired UI built with React and Tailwind CSS. Utilizes Framer Motion for fluid transitions and React Query for aggressive state caching.
