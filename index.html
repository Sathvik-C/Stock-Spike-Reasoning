The Chaos

Academic group chats are a mess of memes, casual conversations, and critical study materials. Important files get buried fast.

  • Scrolling endlessly to find that one PDF
  • Missing assignment deadlines lost in chatter
  • Downloading duplicates of the same notes

The Vault

Export your chat, drop it into StudyVault, and let AI do the heavy lifting. Your study materials are instantly structured.

  • Files categorized automatically (Notes, Exams, etc.)
  • Deadlines and important announcements flagged
  • Universal search across messages and documents

Why StudyVault Stands Out

Designed to solve a real-world problem with modern engineering.

AI-Powered Categorization

Uses Natural Language Processing to understand the context of shared files and automatically sorts them into appropriate folders like Notes, Assignments, or Resources.

Deadline Extraction

The system reads through the noise to identify and flag critical announcements, extracting dates for assignment deadlines and exam schedules.

Universal Search

Stop scrolling. Instantly search across thousands of messages, file names, and extracted text to find exactly what you need in milliseconds.
